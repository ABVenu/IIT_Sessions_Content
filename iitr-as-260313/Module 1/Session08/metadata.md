course name: Certification in Agentic systems and design

target audience: students of any background - not from tech bakground

session duration: 2 hr 15 mins

title: Agent Behavior & Understanding the LLM Layer	

objectuive: Understand how large language models enable agent capabilities	

topics to be covered: how LLMs power agents;LLM capabilities;model providers;very light transformer explanation	

detailed subtopics/learning objectives to be covered: 
* Understand What an LLM Is — Learn what a large language model is in simple terms (text in → text out system);
* Understand LLM’s Role in Agents — See how LLM acts as the “brain” of an agent;
* Explore Basic Capabilities — Understand tasks like answering questions, summarizing, and generating text;
* Observe Input–Output Behavior — See how different inputs produce different outputs using simple examples;
* Understand Prompt as Input — Learn that instructions given to the model control its behavior (no deep prompting yet);
* Identify Strengths of LLMs — Recognize where models work well (language understanding, general knowledge);
* Identify Limitations of LLMs — Understand hallucinations and why models can be confidently wrong;
* Explore Model Providers — Get familiar with providers like OpenAI, Google DeepMind, and Anthropic;
* Understand How Models Are Used — Learn that LLMs are accessed via APIs (conceptual, no coding depth);
* Recognize Token Concept — Understand that text is broken into tokens and why it matters (very high level);
* Introduce Transformer Idea — Learn that LLMs are based on transformer models (intuitive explanation only);
* Understand Attention (Intuition) — See how models “focus” on important words in a sentence;
* Connect LLM to Agent Flow — Understand how model outputs help in reasoning and decision making;
* Analyze Failure Examples — Look at wrong outputs and understand why they happen;
